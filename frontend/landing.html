✦ Core Features

Everything your inbox needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built for engineers, researchers, and power users who demand more from their email client.

🔍

Hybrid RAG Semantic Search

Combines BM25 keyword ranking with NVIDIA 4096-dim dense embeddings using Reciprocal Rank Fusion — finds the right email even from a vague memory.

📄

Document Intelligence

Auto-parses PDFs, PowerPoint slides, and Word documents — extracts up to 12,000 characters of content and generates structured executive summaries with action items and financials.

⚡

Smart Priority Inbox

AI-powered priority scorer surfaces your most important emails at the top — combining recency, sender reputation, attachment presence, and keyword signals.

🔁

Bidirectional Gmail Sync

Every action — archive, star, read, delete — instantly syncs back to your live Gmail account via the Gmail REST API. Your inbox stays consistent across all clients.

🤖

AI Reply & Draft Generation

Generate professional email replies, compose drafts from natural language prompts, and refine tone — all powered by Llama 3.3 70B Instruct via NVIDIA NIM.

🔔

Real-Time Push Webhooks

Google Cloud Pub/Sub webhook listener triggers instant inbox updates — no polling. Supports natural language action commands: "archive all from LinkedIn".

⚙ System Architecture

Enterprise-grade RAG pipeline

A fully decoupled, async architecture designed for real-world scale — from a single developer inbox to thousands of concurrent users.
